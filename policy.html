Privacy Policy for Celebrity Match AI

Last updated: February 12, 2024

Introduction

Celebrity Match AI ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos

  • We temporarily process the photos you take or select for the sole purpose of finding celebrity matches
  • Photos are processed in real-time and are NOT stored on our servers
  • Photos are only stored locally on your device in your app's cache
  • You can delete cached photos at any time by using the "New Photo" button

Camera and Photo Library Access

  • We request access to your device's camera and photo library
  • This access is only used when you explicitly choose to take or select a photo
  • You can control these permissions through your device's settings

Technical Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App performance data
  • Crash reports
  • This information helps us improve the app's functionality

Advertising

  • We use Google AdMob for advertising
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the celebrity matching functionality
  • Improve our app's performance
  • Debug technical issues
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • Photos are processed securely using industry-standard encryption
  • We do not maintain a database of user photos
  • All photo processing is done through secure API calls
  • We use reputable AI services (OpenRouter, Groq) for photo analysis

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • OpenRouter and Groq for AI photo analysis
  • Wikipedia, IMDb, and Instagram for celebrity information
  • Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices

Children's Privacy

  • Our app is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children
  • If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Control app permissions
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
